About Us

Traverse Dynamics Global LLC is a Colorado-based construction consulting firm specializing in high-end tiny homes, modular residential and commercial projects, all utilizing advanced steel framing techniques. Operating out of our uniquely engineered steel Quonset hut headquarters, we combine rugged durability with cutting-edge technology.

Led by Cydney Skeens, a seasoned expert passionate about innovative building solutions, Traverse Dynamics Global blends deep industry experience with a DIY manufacturing approach. We leverage Scottsdale Construction Systems roll formers and state-of-the-art design and engineering software to empower builders and developers to bring smart, sustainable, and upscale structures to life.

Our mission is to help clients navigate the complexities of modern construction with precision, efficiency, and innovation — delivering projects that stand the test of time while reducing cost and environmental impact.
